Welcome to the Union Army, Soldier

The year is 1863. The nation is torn in two — brother fighting brother, neighbor turning against neighbor. You've answered the call of President Lincoln and pledged your allegiance to the Union. Your journey begins now.

This guide will prepare you for the challenges ahead. From the grueling drills of basic training to the mud and misery of camp life, from the stifling wool of your uniform to the roar of cannon fire on the battlefield — every page here is designed to give you a fighting chance.

You'll learn what to eat, what to wear, how to march, and how to survive. Hygiene may be poor, medicine primitive, and comfort scarce — but with the Union behind you and freedom ahead of you, you will endure.

Prepare yourself, recruit. Your country is counting on you.

🗺️ What You'll Find in This Guide

As a Union soldier, preparation is your best weapon. This guidebook is divided into clear sections to help you survive and serve with honor:

  • About the Union Army – Learn about the cause we fight for and the structure of our forces.
  • Enlistment & Training – What it takes to become a soldier, from drills to discipline.
  • Uniform & Equipment – Get to know your gear before you march into battle.
  • Camp Life & Hygiene – Life between the fights: meals, illness, and survival tips.
  • Major Battles & Tactics – Where we've been, how we fight, and why it matters.

New recruits are encouraged to explore each section thoroughly. Your survival — and victory — may depend on it.
